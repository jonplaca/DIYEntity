    Frequently Asked Questions

    Q. What is CompanyDocs.io?

    Our mission is to help simplify the creation, modification, and closure of new/existing business entities. By answering a few questions, we will deliver all the necessary state/federal documents and recommendations to seamlessly manage a business.

    Q. How much does it cost?

    A one-time charge of ten dollars ($10); used mainly to prevent spam submissions while simultanously supporting our backend processes.

    Q. What states do you support?

    Every few weeks we update the service to support new states and their requiments - states are prioritized based on the number of businesses registered within their borders.

    Q. How can I be sure this service is legally sufficient?

    While we are confident in our work, we still suggest working with a lawyer to develop non-State/Federal documents such as Corporate Bylaws. If you would like an introduction to lawyers we trust, please contact us.

    Q. What are some examples of the services?

    By answering a few questions users can: create an domestic entity, establish an foreign entity, convert entity structures (e.g LLC to C-corp), merge with other entities, dissolve an existing business, and much more.

    Q. Why should I use this service?

    Businesses are required, by law, to stay in compliance with State and Federal agencies. Understanding what documents need to be submitted, and when, is a confusing process; at CompanyDocs we provide clear instructions to ensure your business remains compliant.

    Q. What entity structures are supported?

    Mainly Sole Proprietors, Limited Liabilities, Corporations, and Non-profits.

    Terms, Privacy Policy - Please take time to review. If you have any questions, do not hesitate to reach out to us.
